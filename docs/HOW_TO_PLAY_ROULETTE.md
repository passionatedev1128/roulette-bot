# How to Play Brazilian Roulette - Simple Guide

## What is Roulette?

**Roulette** is a casino game where a ball spins around a wheel with numbered pockets (0-36). You bet on which number/color the ball will land on.

---

## The Roulette Wheel

### Numbers on the Wheel:
- **0** (Zero) - Green color
- **1 to 36** - Red or Black colors

### Color Distribution:
- **Red numbers:** 1, 3, 5, 7, 9, 12, 14, 16, 18, 19, 21, 23, 25, 27, 30, 32, 34, 36
- **Black numbers:** 2, 4, 6, 8, 10, 11, 13, 15, 17, 20, 22, 24, 26, 28, 29, 31, 33, 35
- **Green number:** 0 (zero)

---

## How to Play (Step by Step)

### Step 1: Choose Your Bet Amount (Chip Selection)

**At the bottom right of the screen, you see chips:**
- 0.5, 1, 2.5, 5, 20, 50

**What to do:**
1. Click on a chip (e.g., click "5" chip)
2. This selects how much you want to bet

**Example:** Click "5" chip = You're betting 5 units per bet

---

### Step 2: Place Your Bet

**You have betting options on the grid (bottom center):**

#### Option A: Bet on Color (Easiest)

**Bet on Red (Vermelho):**
- Find the "Vermelho" (Red) area on the betting grid
- Click on it
- Your bet is placed on red

**Bet on Black (Preto):**
- Find the "Preto" (Black) area on the betting grid
- Click on it
- Your bet is placed on black

#### Option B: Bet on Specific Number

**Bet on a specific number:**
- Click on any number (0-36) on the betting grid
- Your bet is placed on that number

**Example:** Click number "17" = You bet on number 17

---

### Step 3: Wait for the Spin

**After placing your bet:**
1. A timer counts down (usually 10-30 seconds)
2. During this time, you can place more bets or change your bet
3. When timer reaches 0, no more bets allowed

---

### Step 4: Watch the Result

**The wheel spins and ball lands on a number:**
- Example: Ball lands on number 17 (black)

**The result is displayed:**
- Number: 17
- Color: Black

---

### Step 5: Win or Lose

**If you bet on Black:**
-  **WIN** - You get your bet back + equal profit (1:1 payout)
- Example: Bet 5, win 5, total = 10

**If you bet on Red:**
-  **LOSE** - You lose your bet
- Example: Bet 5, lose 5, total = 0

**If you bet on number 17:**
-  **WIN** - You get bet back + 35x profit (35:1 payout)
- Example: Bet 5, win 175, total = 180

**If you bet on number 25:**
-  **LOSE** - You lose your bet
- Example: Bet 5, lose 5, total = 0

## Common Questions

### Q: Do I need to click a confirm button?
**A:** Usually NO. In Brazilian roulette:
- Click on betting area  Bet is placed
- Timer counts down  Bet is confirmed when timer ends
- No separate "confirm" button needed

### Q: What happens if I click during the spin?
**A:** Usually nothing - betting is closed during spin. You can only bet during the countdown timer.

### Q: Can I change my bet after placing it?
**A:** Yes, during the betting timer. Click "DESFAZER" (Undo) to remove bet, or click different area to change.

### Q: What is "DOBRAR" (Double)?
**A:** Doubles your current bet amount. Example: Bet 5  Click DOBRAR  Bet becomes 10.

### Q: What is "JOGO AUTOMÁTICO" (Auto Play)?
**A:** Plays automatically for multiple rounds. Not needed for the bot.

## Key Concepts

### 1. Betting Timer
- **What:** Countdown before spin
- **Duration:** Usually 10-30 seconds
- **During timer:** You can place bets
- **After timer:** Betting closed, wheel spins

### 2. Payouts
- **Red/Black:** 1:1 (bet 10, win 10)
- **Number:** 35:1 (bet 10, win 350)
- **Zero:** 35:1 (bet 10, win 350)

### 3. Winning
- **Red bet wins:** If ball lands on red number
- **Black bet wins:** If ball lands on black number
- **Number bet wins:** If ball lands on that exact number

### 4. Losing
- **Red bet loses:** If ball lands on black or zero
- **Black bet loses:** If ball lands on red or zero
- **Number bet loses:** If ball lands on any other number
